About Zenith Jiu Jitsu Canada

Zenith Jiu Jitsu Canada is dedicated to providing world class Brazilian jiu jitsu instruction in a welcoming environment for all ages and skill levels.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower students through the art of jiu jitsu, helping them build confidence, discipline and a strong sense of community both on and off the mats.

Founder Story

Zenith Jiu Jitsu Canada was founded by Professor Robert Drysdale, whose dedication to the sport has inspired practitioners across the country. His vision created a network of schools that share the same passion for technical excellence and personal growth.
